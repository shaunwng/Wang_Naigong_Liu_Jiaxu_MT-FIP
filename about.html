Grain

Turbidity in beer creates a complex of proteins and polyphenols, so we select malt with a protein content of less than 12% to produce our beer.

Careful selection

Cooking

Cooking is very important to create a cloudiness-free beer. The rolling during cooking must be very firm, as this helps the proteins denature, coagulate and flocculate.

Not just better beer.

We also brew a better future for all of us.
